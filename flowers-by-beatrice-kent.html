Our Recommended Florist

Blooms That Complete the Moment

Balloons set the scene — but there's something about flowers that makes a celebration feel truly special. The softness of a seasonal bloom next to a balloon arch, or a carefully chosen hand-tied bouquet on a dessert table — it's the kind of detail that photographs beautifully and stays in the memory long after the day.

That's why we're delighted to recommend Flowers by Beatrice, an independent florist based right here in Sittingbourne, creating bespoke fresh floral arrangements for baby showers, birthdays, weddings and celebrations across Kent.

When you book your balloon styling with us, just let us know you'd love flowers too. We'll make sure the colour palette and styling work seamlessly together on the day. Bookings for florals are made directly with Beatrice.

Flowers by Beatrice is based in Sittingbourne and serves the wider Kent area. Known for handcrafted fresh arrangements inspired by nature — beautifully thoughtful, naturally Beatrice.

Why We Recommend Beatrice

Flowers and Balloons,
Better Together

There's a moment when a balloon garland meets a beautiful floral arrangement — and the whole display clicks into place. The texture of a fresh bloom against the smoothness of a balloon arch, or a seasonal centrepiece that ties together the colours of an entire dessert table. It's the kind of detail that makes a celebration feel genuinely considered.

Beatrice's work caught our eye for exactly the same reasons clients choose Little Moment Studio — the care taken over colour, the attention to detail, and the consistency of the finish. She's local to us here in Sittingbourne, which means we can coordinate colours and timing without anything getting lost in translation. When florals and balloons are planned together, the whole event looks like it was designed as one.

We don't earn anything from recommending Beatrice — we simply believe that the celebrations we're part of deserve to be as beautiful as possible from every angle.

How to Book

For florals, get in touch with Beatrice directly — she'll talk you through options, seasonal availability and pricing for your event. Every arrangement is bespoke, so prices vary depending on your chosen stems and scale.

When booking your balloon styling with us, just mention you'd love flowers too and we'll make sure the colour palette and timing work together seamlessly on the day.

Beatrice recommends enquiring as early as possible, particularly for weekend events and peak seasons.
