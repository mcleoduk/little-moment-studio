A Different Kind of Booking

Not just balloons —
a complete vision

The Luxe Baby Shower is our signature setup: a fully designed, fully styled celebration that we build from scratch, so everything is perfect before your guests arrive.

Every element — from the organic balloon installation to the dessert table, the plinths, the props and the florals — is chosen to work together as one cohesive look. Nothing is an afterthought.

Each setup uses our curated neutral palette of Ivory, Nude, Beige, Champagne and Soft Gold, with optional Sage accents — colours chosen because they photograph beautifully and feel genuinely luxurious in any venue.

"We handle everything from arrival to collection — you just walk in, take a breath, and enjoy every moment."
