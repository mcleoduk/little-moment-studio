Deposit & Policy
Collect from all clients at time of booking confirmation. Deposits are non-negotiable.

How Deposits Work

  • Taken at booking, held until prop returned
  • Returned within 48h of safe return
  • Damage deducted at replacement cost
  • No refund if prop returned late without notice
  • Collect deposit before handing over any prop

What Counts as Damage

  • Broken stems or wicker on rattan items
  • Chips, cracks on cake stands / plinths
  • Neon sign cable faults or broken bulbs
  • Stains or soiling on easels or mats
  • Missing parts from multi-piece sets

Booking Rules

  • Min. 1 week notice for all hire items
  • 2 weeks for flower wall & neon signs
  • Sign board wording due 5 days before
  • Props not reserved until deposit paid
  • Same-day bookings at our discretion only

Collection & Return

  • Client collects & returns from studio by default
  • Delivery quoted on request (per mileage)
  • Return by 10am day after event unless agreed
  • Late return: £15/hour charged from deposit
  • Photos taken on handover — both directions
