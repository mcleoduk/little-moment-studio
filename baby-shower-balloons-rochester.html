Venues & homes

Premium balloon styling, delivered to Rochester

We’re based in Sittingbourne — 15 miles from Rochester, around a 20-minute drive along the A2. We travel to Rochester regularly for baby shower installations at venues across the city and surrounding villages.

Rochester is one of Kent’s most characterful cities — a compact historic centre with independent restaurants, a boutique wedding venue, a bold pink café made for celebrations, and pubs with private dining along the High Street. Whether you’re planning an intimate afternoon tea or a fully styled room, there’s a Rochester venue to suit.

Our displays are organic, personal and designed to photograph beautifully. We’ll talk through your colour palette and vision before the day, so when you walk into your venue, everything is exactly as you imagined.

A small delivery charge may apply for Rochester depending on the booking size. We’ll always confirm this upfront when you enquire.

Rochester Venues

Spaces we love working in

Some of the Rochester venues we work with, and what styling works well in each space.

The Vines of Rochester

Boutique Wedding Venue

A stunning boutique wedding venue in the Old Archdeaconry at the heart of Rochester, with 4.7 stars from over 80 reviews. An exclusive-use style event space that makes a baby shower feel genuinely special — historic, elegant and beautifully suited to styled installations.

Styling tip: The historic interior calls for our most considered styling — organic garlands with dried florals, soft neutral tones and pearlescent finishes photograph beautifully against the stone and timber architecture. Our Grand Luxe package was made for a venue like this.

The PINK House

Celebration Café

Rochester’s boldest venue — a vivid pink café designed for celebrations and Instagram-worthy bookings. 4.8 stars and a totally unique aesthetic that makes a baby shower feel joyful and memorable from the moment guests arrive.

Styling tip: Lean into the venue’s energy — bold pinks, pearlescent whites and gold balloon accents look sensational here. A full backdrop installation behind the main table and balloon clusters at the entrance is exactly the right approach. This is a venue where the styling should be joyful, not restrained.

The George Pub & Function Hall

Pub & Function Hall — Free Hire

A well-established pub with a private function hall and garden, offering free hall hire for baby showers. 4.5 stars from over 1,100 reviews. The combination of free venue hire and a flexible, fully decoratable space makes it one of the best-value options in Rochester for a styled celebration.

Styling tip: Free venue hire means your budget goes entirely into styling and food. A full backdrop garland and cake table cluster transforms the hall completely. The flexible layout suits our three-zone setups beautifully — backdrop, cake table and photo corner.

Lenny’s of Rochester

Patisserie & Event Space

A beloved Rochester patisserie on the High Street with a top-floor event space for private celebrations — specifically used for baby showers and small events. 4.5 stars from 170 reviews. A beautiful, intimate setting with the bonus of exceptional in-house patisserie.

Styling tip: The patisserie setting suits refined, delicate styling — soft blush, ivory and sage tones in a focused cake table cluster and photo corner arrangement. The quality of the space (and the pastries) sets the tone. Keep balloon styling elegant and complementary rather than bold.
