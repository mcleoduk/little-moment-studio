Venues & homes

Premium balloon styling, delivered to Maidstone

We're based in Sittingbourne — just 12 miles from Maidstone town centre. We travel to baby shower venues, hotels and private homes across the Maidstone area regularly, and we know the local spaces well.

Maidstone is Kent's county town, and it has some genuinely beautiful venues for baby showers — from countryside barns to elegant hotels. We style them all, from a single garland backdrop to a full room setup covering every focal area.

Our displays are organic, considered and personal. We work closely with you on the colour palette and arrangement before the day, so when you walk into your venue, everything is exactly as you imagined.

A small delivery charge may apply for Maidstone depending on the booking size. We'll always confirm this upfront when you enquire.

Maidstone Venues

Spaces we love working in

Some of the Maidstone venues we've styled, and what works well in each space.

The BarnYard, Lenham

Countryside

A beautiful set of exclusive event spaces in the Kent countryside, just outside Maidstone. The Apple Barn and The Roost both suit organic balloon styling beautifully — exposed beams and natural materials work well with earthy, warm palettes.

Styling tip: Warm cream, sage and gold garlands complement the natural wood interior perfectly. Pampas grass in the arrangement ties the look together.

Mercure Great Danes Hotel

Hotel

A well-established hotel with dedicated event space on the outskirts of Maidstone. Private dining rooms and banqueting areas suit a more formal baby shower setup. The neutral interior works with any colour palette.

Styling tip: The larger function rooms benefit from a substantial backdrop garland — 8ft or more — to fill the wall and create a proper focal point.

Mu Mu Experience

Town Centre

An Italian restaurant in Maidstone town centre that hosts baby showers and gender reveals with a vibrant, social atmosphere. Compact and intimate, which suits a focused styling approach rather than a full room installation.

Styling tip: A cake table cluster and a small photo corner work well here. Soft blush and cream balloons complement the warm interior lighting.

The White Horse, Bearsted

Pub & Restaurant

A popular venue in the village of Bearsted, just east of Maidstone. Private dining areas with a warm, relaxed feel. Suited to more intimate baby showers where the styling can be understated and elegant rather than large-scale.

Styling tip: A single table garland or low-profile cake table cluster works beautifully here — keep it simple and the natural setting does the rest.

Not on the list? We're happy to work at any venue in Maidstone — just mention it when you enquire.
