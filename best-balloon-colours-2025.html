The Best Balloon Colour Palettes for 2025

Little Moment Studio
March 2025
7 min read

Every year, colour sets the tone for celebrations — from the hue of a balloon garland to the ribbon on a cake table. In 2025, the palette world has moved somewhere genuinely exciting: warm, grounded, and quietly luxurious, with bold pops of colour for those who want maximum impact. Whether you're planning a baby shower in Sittingbourne or a first birthday celebration across Kent, here's everything you need to know about the colours that will make your event feel truly current.

We've pulled together the official Pantone forecast, Pinterest's data-driven trend report, and what we're actually seeing from clients here in Kent — so you can walk into your styling consultation knowing exactly what you love.

Pantone Colour of the Year 2025: Mocha Mousse

Each year, the Pantone Colour Institute selects a single shade that captures the cultural mood — and for 2025, that shade is PANTONE 17-1230 Mocha Mousse.

Described by Pantone as evoking "the pleasure and deliciousness of cacao, chocolate, and coffee," Mocha Mousse is a warm, rich brown with grey undertones. It speaks to comfort, quiet luxury, and a desire for depth after years of very pale, Scandi-influenced neutrals. For celebrations, it's having a genuine moment.

The Pinterest 2025 Palette

Pinterest analyses billions of searches each year to identify where consumer taste is heading — and their 2025 palette is one of the most diverse and useful they've published. Five named colours, each with a distinct personality.

Butter Yellow

Soft, warm, and endlessly flattering in photographs — butter yellow has moved firmly into the mainstream for 2025. Pinterest saw search interest rise dramatically, and we can see why. It's the perfect shade for spring and summer baby showers, working beautifully with white, sage green, and soft dusty pink.

Best for: Baby showers · Spring events · Garden parties

Sage Green (and the rise of Dill Green)

Sage has been a staple for two or three years now — and it's not going anywhere. But watch for its successor: Dill Green, a slightly darker, more herby shade that adds genuine depth to arrangements. Think of it as sage's more interesting older sibling.

Best for: Baby showers · Boho celebrations · Mixed with neutral tones

Aura Indigo

A moody, cosmic lilac-indigo that feels genuinely different. Pinterest describes it as ethereal and dreamy — and in balloon form, it creates a truly magical backdrop for first birthday photographs. Works beautifully with iridescent and pearlescent balloon finishes.

Best for: First birthdays · Evening events · Gender-neutral celebrations

Cherry Red

For those who want bold, Cherry Red is having a major moment — Pinterest reports a 325% increase in search interest. It's maximalist, confident, and spectacular in a large balloon installation. Best kept as an accent rather than a base, paired with ivory and gold.

Best for: Milestone birthdays · Bold installations · High-impact events

First Birthday Colours for 2025

First birthday styling in 2025 is splitting into two distinct schools — and both are absolutely valid depending on your personality and venue.

Soft & Dreamy

Iridescent pastels — soft pink, lavender, mint, and baby blue — with a pearlescent sheen and rose gold metallic accents. Aura Indigo sits beautifully in this palette. Monochromatic clusters (all-blush, all-lilac) are particularly sought-after for their photogenic quality. This is our most popular direction for Kent studio shoots.

Bold & High-Energy

Colour-blocked, confident, and joyful. Think teal paired with mustard, or Cherry Red making a statement against gold and ivory. If your little one's personality is already coming through — this is the palette to match it.

Our Top Three Palettes for Sittingbourne & Kent in 2025

Based on what we're seeing from local clients, here are our three favourite complete palettes for the year ahead:

1. Mocha & Gold (Luxury Neutral)

Mocha Mousse · Warm Ivory · Burnished Gold · Alpine Oat. Sophisticated, timeless, and stunning in any venue. Pairs beautifully with dark wood tables and linen.

2. Butter & Sage (Boho Natural)

Butter Yellow · Sage Green · Dill Green · Dusty Pink · Warm Cream. The 2025 update on the boho palette. Particularly beautiful for outdoor or garden celebrations.

3. Aura Indigo & Blush (Dreamy Ethereal)

Aura Indigo · Iridescent Blush · Lavender · Rose Gold metallic. Magical for first birthdays and evening celebrations. The pearlescent balloon finishes elevate it beyond the ordinary.
