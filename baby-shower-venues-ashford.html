The Best Baby Shower Venues Near Ashford: A Local Guide

Little Moment Studio
May 2026
10 min read

Ashford is one of the fastest-growing towns in Kent — and with that growth has come a genuinely impressive range of places to host a baby shower. From characterful independent tearooms in the town centre and Kennington to village halls with plenty of space, and a full-service hotel with event suites, there’s something here for every style, budget and group size.

We travel to Ashford regularly from our studio in Sittingbourne, and over time we’ve built a clear picture of which spaces work well and why. This guide covers ten of our top recommendations, along with our honest notes on each venue for baby shower balloon styling. Prices shown are guides only — always confirm current rates directly with the venue.

What to Look For in a Venue — Before You Book

Most people focus on capacity and cost when choosing a venue. Those matter — but there are a few other things worth checking, especially if you’re planning professional balloon styling.

Our Venue Checklist

Ceiling height — Low ceilings suit garlands and table displays; high ceilings allow tall arches and more dramatic installations. Ask before assuming.
Early access — We need at least 60–90 minutes before guests arrive to set up properly. Confirm whether your hire includes setup time.
Decoration policy — Some venues restrict tape or fixings on walls. We always have freestanding alternatives, but it’s worth knowing in advance.
Natural light — Daylight makes photos look beautiful. Ashford’s Kennington venues and the hotel offer particularly good natural light in the event spaces.
Parking — Town-centre venues have multi-storey car parks nearby; out-of-town venues in Kennington tend to have easier roadside or on-site parking.
Catering flexibility — Can you bring your own food, or do you need to use the venue’s caterers? Important for anyone wanting a specific menu or cake.

“Tell your venue you’re planning professional balloon styling when you enquire. It opens a useful conversation about ceiling height, fixings and access that saves time later.”

Getting Around Ashford

Ashford town centre has good parking at the Park Mall and Elwick Place multi-storey car parks — both close to the town-centre venues. For venues in Kennington (Williams & Thomas, Harvester Bybrook Barn and The Kennington Cafe) there’s generally easier roadside parking available.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for in a baby shower venue near Ashford?

Beyond capacity and cost, the things that matter most are ceiling height (which determines what balloon displays are possible), early access for setup, whether the venue allows decorations, parking, and natural light for photos. Village halls like Singleton and Godinton give you a blank canvas and full control, while tearooms and hotel event suites offer a more complete experience with catering included.

How much does venue hire cost for a baby shower near Ashford?

Village halls in the area typically charge on an hourly rate. Tearooms like The Teapot and Williams & Thomas typically work on a minimum spend or set menu basis. Pubs like The New Chimneys hire private rooms separately. The Ashford International Hotel event suites are quoted per event — contact their events team for current pricing.

Can I bring my own decorations to venues near Ashford?

Most village halls in the area allow you to bring your own decorations. Always check with the venue first — some have restrictions on tape or fixings on walls. We’re experienced at working within venue rules and can always find a display approach that works without damaging the space.

How early do I need access for balloon styling near Ashford?

We recommend at least 60–90 minutes before guests arrive. When you book, mention you’re planning professional balloon styling — it’s a useful conversation to have early and helps everyone plan access times correctly.
