Handcrafted Balloon Garlands, Designed for You

Balloon garlands are our most popular service — and for good reason. They're versatile, show-stopping and completely bespoke. Whether draped across a table, framing a doorway or cascading down a staircase, a garland transforms any space instantly.

Every garland is built by hand using premium latex balloons in your chosen colour palette. We mix sizes, textures and shapes to create an organic, natural flow that looks effortless — but is anything but.

Our garlands typically range from 2 to 6 metres. Tell us your space and we'll recommend the perfect size and style.

Balloon Garland FAQs

Balloon garlands start from £150 for a 2-metre display. Larger garlands and full arches are priced based on size and complexity. We'll provide a clear quote after your free consultation.
A garland is a flowing, organic arrangement that drapes along surfaces — tables, walls, staircases. An arch is a freestanding structure that creates a doorway or backdrop shape. Both use the same organic balloon styling, just in different formations.
Our air-filled garlands typically last 2–3 days indoors. We recommend setting up on the day of your event for the best results. Outdoor garlands are possible but best suited to sheltered areas away from direct sunlight and wind.
