Birthday Balloon
Styling Across Kent

From first birthdays to milestone celebrations, we create stunning balloon displays that make every birthday unforgettable. Based in Sittingbourne, styling parties across Kent.

Every Birthday Deserves to Feel Special

Whether it's a little one turning one or a landmark birthday worth celebrating in style, our balloon installations create the perfect backdrop for your party.

We specialise in birthday balloon garlands, organic arches, number displays and themed setups — all designed around your colour scheme, venue and vision. No two celebrations are ever the same.

From design to setup and collection, we take care of everything. You focus on the cake and the candles — we'll handle the rest.
