We're based in Sittingbourne — around 15 miles from Rochester and Chatham. We travel regularly to birthday parties across Medway, from private homes in Gillingham and Strood to hired halls and hotels throughout Rochester and Chatham Maritime.

Medway Venues

Spaces we love working in

Some of the Medway venues we style for birthdays, and what works well in each space.

Bridgewood Manor Hotel, Walderslade

Hotel

A well-appointed hotel set in landscaped grounds on the edge of Chatham. The dedicated function suites are a popular choice for milestone birthdays — clean, neutral interiors that work beautifully with any colour palette.

Styling tip: The larger suites take a generous backdrop garland well. Metallics — gold, champagne, rose gold — photograph brilliantly in this setting and suit milestone celebrations particularly well.

Chatham Historic Dockyard

Historic Venue

One of Medway's most dramatic event spaces. The Dockyard's various private hire buildings offer an extraordinary backdrop that makes even simple balloon styling look spectacular — especially for older milestone birthdays.

Styling tip: Keep the palette sophisticated to match the setting — warm metallics, dusty rose or earthy neutrals. Let the architecture do some of the work; the balloons frame rather than fill.

Cooling Castle Barn, Cooling

Countryside Barn

A beautiful countryside barn on the Hoo Peninsula north of Rochester. Exposed beams, stone walls and natural light make it a wonderful setting for birthday parties — rustic and atmospheric without being rough around the edges.

Styling tip: Warm, organic tones — cream, terracotta, sage — are made for this space. A long draping garland along the main beam is always a showstopper here.

Private Homes, Medway

Home Party

Many of our Medway birthday bookings are at private homes across Rochester, Chatham, Gillingham and Strood — gardens, living rooms and dining rooms transformed for the occasion. Home parties are often the most personal and the most photographed.

Styling tip: A focused cake table cluster plus a garland above works better than spreading styling thin across a smaller space. Let the display be the hero of the room.

Not on the list? We're happy to work at any venue across Medway — just mention it when you enquire.
