Gender Reveal Balloons in Maidstone

Venues & homes

The reveal moment, designed beautifully

We're based in Sittingbourne — just 12 miles from Maidstone town centre. Gender reveals are some of our favourite celebrations to style, because everything builds towards a single, unrepeatable moment: the one where everyone finds out together.

Getting that moment right matters. The backdrop, the colour palette, the way the reveal station is positioned for photographs — every detail shapes how the announcement feels and how it will be remembered.

We're a celebration styling studio, not a balloon shop. Every display we create is designed around your reveal method, your colour palette and your space — so when the moment arrives, everything is exactly as it should be.

A small delivery charge may apply for Maidstone depending on booking size — we'll always confirm this upfront when you enquire.

Styling Directions

Popular gender reveal themes

Every display is bespoke — but these are the directions our clients love most.

Elegant Neutrals

Soft cream, ivory and champagne gold. A sophisticated reveal palette that keeps the focus entirely on the moment itself — not the colour.

Classic Pink & Blue

The timeless reveal palette. Both colours present until the moment — then everything makes sense. Clean, joyful and instantly understood by every guest.

Sage & White

Modern, photogenic and popular for garden and outdoor reveals. The soft green palette works beautifully in natural light and photographs crisply.

Teddy Bear

Warm browns, cream and gold. Sweet, classic and enduring — one of our most requested themes for expectant parents who want something timeless rather than trend-led.

Boho Natural

Terracotta, dusty rose, pampas and warm beige. Relaxed and contemporary — particularly beautiful against natural textures and outdoor settings.
