Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

Little Moment Studio is a celebration styling business based at Forum Shopping Centre, Sittingbourne, ME10 3DL. We are the data controller for the personal information you share with us.

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@littlemomentstudio.co.uk or call 07700 900 123.

What Information We Collect

When you get in touch with us — through the contact form on our website, by email, or by phone — we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Details about your event (date, venue, type of celebration)
  • Any other information you choose to share with us

We do not collect any payment card details through our website. Payments are handled separately and securely.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and discuss your celebration
  • Prepare and send your design proposal
  • Manage your booking, including deposit and balance payments
  • Contact you in connection with your event

We will never sell your information to third parties or use it for unsolicited marketing without your permission.

Our Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds under UK GDPR:

  • Contract — where processing is necessary to fulfil a booking you have made with us
  • Legitimate interests — where we have a genuine business reason to process your data, such as responding to an enquiry, and this does not override your rights
  • Consent — where you have given us explicit permission, such as agreeing to receive further communications from us

How Long We Keep Your Data

If you make an enquiry that does not result in a booking, we will retain your information for up to 12 months, after which it will be securely deleted.

If you become a client, we will retain your booking information for up to 3 years for legitimate business purposes (such as accounting records), after which it will be securely deleted.

Third Parties

Our website is hosted by Vercel, which may process limited technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of providing hosting services. Vercel is a reputable provider and processes data in accordance with GDPR.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties unless required to do so by law.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to our processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@littlemomentstudio.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — the UK's data protection authority — at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

We would always appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns directly before you contact the ICO, so please do reach out to us first.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to check back periodically.
