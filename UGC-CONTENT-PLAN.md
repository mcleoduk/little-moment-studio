# UGC & Video Content Plan — Little Moment Studio

Target frequency: **weekly short-form** (Reels/TikTok) + **fortnightly long-form** (YouTube/journal embed)

Platform strategy:
- **Instagram Reels & TikTok** — primary distribution, short-form (15–60 sec)
- **YouTube** — longer tutorials, time-lapses, embeddable in journal articles
- **Journal (littlemomentstudio.co.uk)** — embed YouTube video + written article for SEO

- [ ] Clean, uncluttered background for build videos

## Content Types & Format Guide

| Type | Length | Style | Goal |
|------|--------|-------|------|
| **Time-lapse build** | 15–30 sec | Fixed camera, sped up, music | Satisfying, shareable |
| **Kit/tools reveal** | 30–45 sec | Flat lay or handheld, voiceover | Builds authority |
| **Tutorial** | 5–10 min | YouTube, narrated steps | SEO, journal embed |
| **Behind the scenes** | 30–60 sec | Casual, handheld | Trust & personality |
| **Trend/inspo** | 30–45 sec | Slides or quick cuts | Saves/shares |

## Notes

- Always film the finished setup as well as the build — gives you two pieces of content from one job
- Vertical 9:16 for Reels/TikTok, horizontal 16:9 for YouTube
- CapCut is free and good for speeding up time-lapses and adding text
- When a video goes on YouTube, write the journal article to embed it — double the SEO value
- Tag location (Sittingbourne, Kent) in every post
