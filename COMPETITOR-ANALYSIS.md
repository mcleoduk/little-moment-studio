## Competitor Details

### Kent Occasions (kent-occasions.co.uk/balloons/)

- **Strengths:** Good H1, decent content length (~1200 words), covers multiple balloon types
- **Weaknesses:** No meta description, no schema, no image alt text, single page for all services
- **Services:** Garlands, 3ft balloons, walls, bubble balloons, clusters, ceiling, hoops, columns, centrepieces

### The Glitzy Balloon Company (theglitzyballooncompany.co.uk)
- **Location:** Kent area
- **Strengths:** Has Article schema markup, dedicated pages per service
- **Weaknesses:** Very thin content (258 words), no image alt text, emoji in meta description, weak H1
- **Services:** Baby showers, gender reveals, balloon walls, backdrops

### Trix Luxe Designs (trixluxedesigns.co.uk)
- **Location:** Swale, Kent — covers Kent, London, Surrey, Essex
- **Strengths:** Good H1, decent content, FAQ section on page, competitive pricing
- **Weaknesses:** No meta description, no schema, generic image alt text ("gallery image 1")

### BishBoshBash Balloons (bishboshbashballoons.co.uk)
- **Location:** Maidstone, Kent

- **Weaknesses:** No meta description, no schema, no image alt text, messy title tag, all on one page
- **Services:** Helium balloons, arches, columns, sequin walls, flower walls

### Balloonatic (balloonatic.co.uk)
- **Location:** Sheerness, Kent (Russell Street) — 30+ year family business
- **Model:** E-commerce / click & collect + local delivery

- **Weaknesses:** Poor title tag ("Home - Balloonatic"), meta description only in schema not in HTML head, generic/meaningless image alt text ("34", "Elf House Arrival"), thin homepage content (~300-400 words), no blog
- **Services:** Balloon bouquets, helium letters/numbers, party decorations, banners, tableware, LED number hire, cake accessories

## Where Little Moment Studio Wins

1. **Schema breadth** — We have LocalBusiness + Service + FAQ + BlogPosting schema. Only Balloonatic and Balloon Sensations have any schema at all, and neither has Service or FAQ schema. Google rewards this with rich snippets.
2. **FAQ schema** — Not a single competitor has FAQ JSON-LD. We do. This means potential rich snippet real estate on search results that no one else is claiming.
3. **Dedicated keyword landing pages** — Most competitors dump everything on one page. We have 3 targeted landing pages plus a blog post, each optimised for different keyword clusters.
4. **Image alt text** — Across all 9 competitors, none have properly keyword-optimised alt text. Balloonatic uses meaningless text ("34"), Trix Luxe uses "gallery image 1". Ours are keyword-rich and location-specific.
5. **Blog content** — Zero competitors are publishing blog content. Our colour trends article targets long-tail searches they'll never rank for.
6. **Meta descriptions** — 6 out of 9 competitors are missing meta descriptions entirely. Google auto-generates poor snippets for them, giving us better click-through rates.
7. **Competitor attrition** — 5 out of 13 Kent balloon businesses have broken/offline websites right now. Their search traffic is up for grabs.
8. **Sitemap** — Submitted and structured. Most competitors either don't have one or rely on auto-generated (Wix/WordPress) versions with no priority weighting.

## Where Competitors Are Ahead

1. **Domain age** — they're indexed and have been live for years
2. **Backlinks** — established businesses have links from directories, suppliers, venues
3. **Google reviews** — some have accumulated reviews over time
4. **Social following** — Boutique Balloons Kent has strong Instagram/Facebook presence
5. **Schema variety** — Balloonatic and Go2Balloons have Organization + Breadcrumb schemas we don't yet have (worth adding in Phase 2)
6. **Service range** — Balloon Sensations offers LED numbers, shimmer walls, bubble houses. The Mini Event Co offers soft play. Diversified services capture more search terms.

## Closing the Gap

- Get domain live and indexed ASAP
- Set up Google My Business immediately
- Collect Google reviews from first customers
- Submit to local directories (see quick wins list below)
- Post monthly blog content to build authority
- Build social presence on Instagram
