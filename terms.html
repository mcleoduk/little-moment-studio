About Us

Little Moment Studio is a celebration styling business trading from Forum Shopping Centre, Sittingbourne, ME10 3DL. These Terms & Conditions apply to all bookings and services provided by Little Moment Studio.

By making a booking with us, you confirm that you have read and agree to these terms. If you have any questions, please contact us at hello@littlemomentstudio.co.uk before booking.

Bookings & Deposits

To secure your booking and reserve your event date, a non-refundable deposit of 25% of the total agreed price is required at the time of booking.

Your date is not confirmed until the deposit has been received in full. We reserve the right to offer your date to other clients until the deposit is paid.

The remaining balance is due 14 days before your event. If the balance is not received by this date, we reserve the right to cancel the booking. The deposit will be retained in such circumstances.

Cancellations

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 14 days before your event — the balance (if already paid) will be refunded in full. The booking deposit is non-refundable and will be retained.
  • Within 14 days of your event — as materials will have been ordered and preparations made, the deposit is retained and no further charges are made. Any balance already paid will be refunded.

In some circumstances, we may be able to transfer your booking to a new date, subject to availability. Please get in touch as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Prop Hire

Where props (such as backdrops, stands, frames, or decorative pieces) are included as part of your styling package, a refundable damage deposit is required in addition to the package price. The amount will be confirmed in your design proposal.

Props remain the property of Little Moment Studio at all times. You are responsible for the safe keeping of hired items from the point of delivery/setup to the point of collection.

The damage deposit will be returned promptly following collection, provided items are returned in good condition and free from damage. Damage, loss, or failure to return items may result in the partial or full retention of the deposit, or an additional charge where the cost of replacement exceeds the deposit amount.

Props are available as part of a full styling package only and are not available for standalone hire.

Where a power supply is required for your installation, you are responsible for ensuring a suitable socket is available nearby. We will advise you of this requirement in advance.

We take every care with our installations, but we cannot accept liability for damage to walls, floors, ceilings, or other surfaces where fixings or adhesives are required. We will always discuss fixing methods with you in advance.

Travel

We currently operate within a 10-mile radius of our base at Forum Shopping Centre, Sittingbourne. Travel within this area is included in the quoted price.

For events outside this area, please contact us to discuss availability and any associated travel costs.
