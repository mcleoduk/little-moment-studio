Booking & Deposits

To confirm your date, we ask for a 25% non-refundable booking deposit at the time of enquiry. Once received, your date is reserved and we begin planning your celebration together. The remaining balance is due 14 days before your event.

Once your booking is confirmed, we begin sourcing your bespoke materials, reserving your date in our calendar, and dedicating design time to your celebration. The deposit covers this work and ensures your date isn't offered to anyone else.

We completely understand that life doesn't always go to plan. If you need to cancel:

  • More than 14 days before your event — your balance is refunded in full; only the booking deposit is retained.
  • Within 14 days of your event — as materials will have been ordered and preparations made, the deposit is retained and no further charge is made.

In some circumstances, we may be able to transfer your booking to a new date, subject to availability — just get in touch and we'll do our very best to help.

Please get in touch and we'll see what we can arrange.
