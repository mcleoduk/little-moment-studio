The Best Birthday Party Venues in Maidstone: A Local Guide

Little Moment Studio
June 2026
9 min read

Maidstone is one of the most popular areas we travel to from our Sittingbourne studio for children’s birthday parties — and the choice of venues genuinely reflects that. From farm-based parties to gymnastics clubs, activity centres and hotel function rooms, there’s a strong range of options whatever age you’re celebrating.

This guide covers six venues we’d recommend for a children’s birthday party in Maidstone, along with our notes on what each space is like for birthday balloon styling in Maidstone. Prices and details are guides only — always confirm current rates and availability directly with the venue.

What to Look For in a Venue — Before You Book

Capacity and cost are the obvious things to check, but a few other details make a real difference once you start planning styling and decorations.

Our Venue Checklist

Ceiling height — Low ceilings suit garlands and table displays; high ceilings allow tall arches and floating installations. Ask before assuming.
Early access — We need at least 60–90 minutes before guests arrive to set up properly. Confirm whether your hire includes setup time.
Decoration policy — Activity-based venues often handle their own theming as part of the package — check what’s included before adding extra balloon styling.
Structured vs. blank-space — Decide whether you want a venue that runs the party for you (soft play, farm, gymnastics) or a hall you style and organise yourself.
Parking — Easy to overlook, but guests arriving with children and presents will appreciate it.
Catering — Some venues include food as part of the package; others expect you to bring your own or arrange external catering.

“Tell your venue you’re planning professional balloon styling when you enquire. It opens a useful conversation about ceiling height, fixings and access that saves time later.”

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for in a birthday party venue in Maidstone?

Beyond capacity and cost, check ceiling height (which affects what balloon displays are possible), how early you can access the venue, whether decorations are allowed, parking, and whether the venue runs structured activities or is a blank space you style yourself. Our checklist above covers all of these.

How much does it cost to hire a birthday party venue in Maidstone?

Activity-based parties are usually priced as a package — Lower Grange Farm starts from around £160 for a one-hour activity plus party room time. Community hall hire tends to be the most affordable option if you’re organising catering and decorations yourself. Hotel and premium event spaces are typically quoted per event.

Can I bring my own decorations to Maidstone venues?

Hall hire and hotel function rooms generally welcome your own decorations. Activity-based venues like soft play, farm parties and gymnastics clubs usually include their own theming as part of the package, so it’s worth checking what’s already provided before booking additional balloon styling.

How early do I need access to set up balloon styling?

We recommend at least 60–90 minutes before guests arrive for a hall or function room. Activity-based venues with fixed time slots often have less flexibility, so ask when booking whether early access for styling is possible.
