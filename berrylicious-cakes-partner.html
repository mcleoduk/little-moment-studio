Our Recommended Cake Maker

The Perfect Finishing Touch

We believe the best celebrations are the ones where every detail feels considered — from the balloon garland above the cake table right down to the cupcakes sitting on it.

That's why we're delighted to recommend Berrylicious — Cakes by Jacqui, an independent cake maker based in Rainham creating handmade, bespoke cakes and cupcakes for birthdays, baby showers, weddings and special occasions.

When you book your balloon styling with us, just let us know you'd love cakes too. We'll make sure the styling works beautifully together on the day. Bookings for cakes are made directly with Jacqui.

Berrylicious is based in Rainham and serves the wider Medway and Kent area. Known for detailed fondant work, elegant buttercream finishes and beautifully presented themed collections.

Why We Recommend Berrylicious

The Same Attention to Detail,
Every Single Time

Jacqui's work caught our eye for exactly the same reasons clients choose Little Moment Studio — the attention to detail, the thoughtful colour palettes and the care taken to make every order feel genuinely personal.

Her themed cupcake collections in particular pair beautifully with balloon garlands and dessert table setups. The fondant work is intricate without being fussy, and the presentation is consistently beautiful. We've found they make the whole display feel complete in a way that's hard to put into words until you see it.

We don't earn anything from recommending Berrylicious — we simply think Jacqui is brilliant at what she does, and we want the celebrations we're part of to feel as wonderful as possible from every angle.

How to Book

For cakes and cupcakes, get in touch with Jacqui directly — she'll talk you through options, pricing and timescales for your specific event.

When booking your balloon styling or dessert table setup with us, just mention you're using Berrylicious and we'll make sure everything works beautifully together on the day.

Jacqui recommends booking in advance where possible, especially for weekends and seasonal events.
